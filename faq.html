Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and approach to cultural transformation.

Our approach combines quantitative diagnostics with qualitative insights and co-created interventions. We blend evidence-led methodologies with people-centered design, ensuring changes are measurable, sustainable, and tailored to your organization's specific context.

Transformation timelines vary based on organizational size and complexity. Our assessment phase typically takes 4-6 weeks, with implementation roadmaps spanning 6-18 months. We emphasize sustainable change over quick fixes, with ongoing support available.

We work across multiple sectors including healthcare, technology, finance, education, and manufacturing. Our principles of cultural transformation are universal, though we tailor our approach to each industry's specific challenges and regulatory environments.

We use a combination of quantitative metrics (employee engagement scores, retention rates, productivity measures) and qualitative indicators (leadership feedback, cultural artifacts analysis). Baseline measurements are established during assessment and progress is tracked at regular intervals.

Our process follows four key phases:

  1. Discovery & Assessment (4-6 weeks)
  2. Strategy Development & Alignment (2-4 weeks)
  3. Implementation & Training (3-12 months)
  4. Sustainability & Evaluation (ongoing)
